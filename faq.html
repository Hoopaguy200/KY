FAQ

Q. What is blind spot bias, and why is it important to address?
A. Blind spot bias is a cognitive bias where individuals are unaware of their own biases but easily recognize them in others. It's important to address because it affects decision-making, relationships, and self-awareness, often leading to unfair judgments or poor choices.
Q. What is the purpose of the blind spot bias bot?
A. The bot is designed to help users become more aware of their own cognitive biases, especially blind spot bias. It provides gentle prompts, reflective questions, and examples to encourage self-examination and improve critical thinking.
Q. How does the bot help detect blind spot bias in users?
A. The bot analyzes user responses to questions or scenarios and looks for patterns that suggest biased thinking. It then offers feedback or alternative perspectives to encourage users to reconsider their assumptions.
